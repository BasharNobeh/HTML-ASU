About Us

Our Story

We are a group of passionate youth based in Amman, Jordan who came together to start this bookstore project. Our aim is to bring literature closer to everyone by expanding our presence online.Located in the heart of Amman, our bookstore is not just a place to find books but a hub for book lovers to connect, share stories, and inspire each other.

At The Books Shop, our mission is simple yet profound – to be the bridge between readers and the vast universe of literature. We're not just selling books; we're cultivating a space where stories become companions, ideas ignite, and the joy of reading is celebrated. Our goal is to curate a diverse collection that resonates with every reader, fostering a community bound by the shared love for the written word. Join us in this literary journey, where each page turned opens a door to new worlds. Welcome to a place where stories find a home – welcome to The Books Shop Jo !.

We're delighted to have you as part of our literary community at The Books Shop. As you embark on this exciting journey with us, may each book you discover open doors to new realms of imagination and inspiration. Happy reading!
